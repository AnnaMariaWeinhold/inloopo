Which ETF do I use to implement the strategy?

I implement the strategy with the SPDR S&P 500 EUR Hedged UCITS ETF (ISIN: IE00BYYW2V44, WKN: A2AGXP).

If you want to use another ETF, make sure that the currency risks against the US dollar are hedged. You can recognise this by the ETF name suffix "EUR Hedged".

If your national currency is not the Euro but the British Pound, choose an ETF that hedges the currency risks against the GBP (e.g. GBP Hedged).
